# 🚗 EV Market Intelligence Dashboard | India Sales & Adoption Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

India's electric vehicle (EV) market is experiencing rapid growth, creating opportunities for manufacturers, policymakers, investors, and mobility service providers. However, understanding adoption patterns across vehicle segments and states remains a challenge.

This project analyzes EV sales data across India to uncover market trends, regional demand patterns, vehicle segment performance, and growth opportunities. Using Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI, an interactive business intelligence dashboard was developed to transform raw sales data into actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Stakeholders in the EV ecosystem require data-driven insights to answer critical questions:

* Which states are leading EV adoption?
* Which vehicle segments dominate the market?
* How has EV demand evolved over time?
* Which vehicle classes contribute the most to total sales?
* Where are the future growth opportunities within India's EV market?

This project addresses these questions through exploratory data analysis and interactive dashboarding.

## 📈 Key Insights

* 2-Wheelers contribute approximately 50% of total EV sales.
* 3-Wheelers account for approximately 45% of overall EV adoption.
* Uttar Pradesh is the leading EV market by sales volume.
* Maharashtra, Karnataka, Delhi, and Rajasthan are among the strongest EV adoption markets.
* E-Rickshaws and E-Scooters are among the highest-performing vehicle classes.
* EV adoption has accelerated significantly in recent years, indicating strong market expansion.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Market Expansion

* Focus on high-growth states with increasing EV adoption.
* Replicate successful strategies from top-performing regions.

### Product Strategy

* Continue investment in 2-Wheeler and 3-Wheeler segments due to strong market demand.
* Explore growth opportunities within underpenetrated 4-Wheeler categories.

### Policy & Infrastructure

* Expand charging infrastructure in emerging markets.
* Support incentives for vehicle classes showing high growth potential.
